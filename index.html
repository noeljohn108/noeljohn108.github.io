My Goals After High School

After high school, I want to study engineering. I’m interested in learning how computers and digital systems work, including concepts like:

  • Bit: A binary digit, either 0 or 1.
  • Byte: A sequence of 8 bits.
  • Roundoff: Error that occurs when numbers can’t be represented precisely with limited bits.
  • Analog data: Values that change smoothly over time, like pitch and volume in music.
  • Lossless: Data compression that allows full recovery of the original.
  • Lossy: Data compression that discards some data, so it can’t be fully recovered.
  • Metadata: Data about data, like info about a file or database row.
